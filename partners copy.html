Partners & Sponsors

MedTech Imperial brings together engineers, medics, scientists and entrepreneurs to solve real healthcare challenges. Our partners sit at the heart of that mission – co-creating events, projects and opportunities for the next generation of MedTech leaders.

Become a partner View society page

Why partner with MedTech Imperial?

As Imperial College London’s MedTech society, we connect industry and academia with a highly motivated talent pool spanning medicine, engineering, computing and life sciences.

We work with partners on sponsorship, events, project support and talent initiatives tailored to your goals.

Access top talent

Reach students genuinely interested in MedTech, digital health and healthcare innovation across undergraduate and postgraduate programmes.

Showcase innovation

Share your work through talks, panels and technical workshops that highlight your products, research and technology stack.

Support real projects

Engage with student teams working on MedTech projects, hackathons and early–stage venture ideas.

Build your brand

Strengthen your campus presence through long–term collaboration, branding at flagship events and digital promotion.
